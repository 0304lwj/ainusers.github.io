环境准备

                        
    
1.activemq安装包：https://archive.apache.org/dist/activemq/5.14.0/apache-activemq-5.14.0-bin.tar.gz
2.activemq官网下载：http://activemq.apache.org/activemq-5140-release.html
3.注意activemq需要依赖jdk环境

安装环境

                        
    
0.依赖的jdk环境，可以参考这篇文章搭建：http://www.ainusers.top/html/sixiang/huanjing/jdk/jdk.html
1.下载activemq安装包
    wget https://archive.apache.org/dist/activemq/5.14.0/apache-activemq-5.14.0-bin.tar.gz
2.解压下载activemq安装包压缩包
    tar -zxvf apache-activemq-5.14.0-bin.tar.gz
3.设置环境变量(含jdk环境设置)
    # java_profile
    export JAVA_HOME=/usr/local/tools/jdk1.8
    export PATH=$JAVA_HOME/bin:$PATH
    export CLASSPATH=.:$JAVA_HOME/lib/dt.jar:$JAVA_HOME/lib/tools.jar
    # activemq_profile
    export ACTIVEMQ_HOME=/usr/local/tools/apache-activemq-5.14.0
    export ACTIVEMQ_BIN=$ACTIVEMQ_HOME/bin
    export PATH=${PATH}:${ACTIVEMQ_BIN}
4.启动activemq
    ./activemq start
5.关闭防火墙
    service iptables stop
6.访问activemq
    http://192.168.1.157:8161/admin/
7.默认用户信息
    admin/admin
8.此时成功登陆activemq界面
